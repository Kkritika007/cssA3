Interesting Facts About Reese's

Inventor's Background: H.B. Reese, the creator of Reese's, was a former dairy farmer and worked for Milton S. Hershey before inventing the peanut butter cups. Basement Beginnings: Reese started selling his peanut butter cups from his basement in the 1920s. Initially, they were a penny candy. Hershey's Acquisition: In 1963, the H.B. Reese Candy Company was sold to The Hershey Company, making Reese's a part of the Hershey's family. Iconic Commercials: The brand became famous for its commercials, including the memorable "You got your chocolate in my peanut butter! You got your peanut butter in my chocolate!" campaign.
